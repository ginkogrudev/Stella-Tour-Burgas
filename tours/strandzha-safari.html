About this Trip

Experience the wild side of the Black Sea coast! We take you deep into the Strandzha Nature Park in our 4x4 Jeeps. You will see hidden waterfalls, ancient Thracian sanctuaries, and enjoy a traditional Bulgarian lunch in a forest village. This is not a bus tour; this is a real adventure.

Itinerary

1

09:00 - Pickup

We pick you up directly from your hotel in Sunny Beach or Burgas.

2

10:30 - Off-Road Action

Enter the forest tracks. Stop at the "Mladejko" waterfall.

3

13:00 - Traditional Lunch

BBQ lunch in a forest hut with local wine and honey tasting.
