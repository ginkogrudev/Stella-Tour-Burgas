Discover Istanbul

You cannot see Istanbul in one day. Stay the night and experience the magic of the Bosphorus at sunset. We combine guided sightseeing of the historical Sultanahmet area with free time for shopping and leisure.

Itinerary

1

Day 1: Arrival & History

Departure 05:00. Arrival in Istanbul. Panorama bus tour. Visit the Iron Church "St. Stefan". Check-in at Hotel. Evening: Optional "Gar" music hall or Bosphorus Boat.

2

Day 2: Palaces & Shopping

Breakfast at hotel. Optional visit to Dolmabahçe Palace or Sky Scraper "Sapphire". Afternoon stop at Forum Istanbul Mall. Return to Burgas ~21:00.
