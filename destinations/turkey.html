Good to know before you go

🤝

Bargaining is expected

In markets like the Grand Bazaar or the Blue Market in Edirne, never accept the first price. Negotiate politely with a smile.

🕌

Mosque Etiquette

Women must cover their heads, shoulders, and knees. Men should wear long pants. Shoes are always removed before entering.

💳

Cards vs. Cash

Hotels and large stores accept cards everywhere. For street food and open markets, always carry Turkish Lira (TRY) in cash.

🗣️

Basic Words

  • Hello: Merhaba
  • Thank you: Teşekkür ederim
  • How much?: Ne kadar?
