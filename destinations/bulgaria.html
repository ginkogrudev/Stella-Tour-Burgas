Good to know before you go

🍷

Wine & Dine

Don't miss trying the local wines in the Ivaylovgrad region or traditional slow-cooked meals in the Balkan mountains.

🧥

Mountain Climate

If visiting Tryavna or Hut Zdravets, always pack a light jacket. Even in mid-summer, evenings in the mountains can be crisp.

💳

Cards vs. Cash

Hotels accept cards everywhere. However, carrying Leva (BGN) is highly recommended for small village taverns and buying souvenirs.

🩴

SPA Etiquette

While 4-star hotels provide robes and towels, it is standard practice in Bulgaria to bring your own clean flip-flops for the pool area.
